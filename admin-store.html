Automation Workflows

Configure your automated email and CRM triggers.

🛒 Abandoned Cart Recovery

Trigger: User adds items to cart but doesn't purchase within 2 hours.

Action: Sends a POST request with cart data to your configured webhook (n8n / Zapier).

✅ Purchase Confirmation

Trigger: Customer completes checkout successfully.

Action: Sends order receipt to customer + internal notification to your admin email.

📋 Lead Registration (CRM)

Trigger: Someone submits the contact form on the How to Buy page.

Action: Adds lead to CRM with tag "Web Lead" and sends a welcome email.

💡 How to connect: Each workflow sends a structured JSON payload to your webhook URL. Create your automation flows in n8n or Zapier, copy your webhook URL above, toggle the switch ON, and click Save Settings.
